About Maison d’Essence

Founded in Glasgow in 2022, Maison d’Essence was created to distill the soul of nature into its purest form.
From flower fields to steam, from lab to bottle, our mission is to sustainably preserve fragrance for the world’s finest creators.

Rebecca – Quality Assurance Manager 🐱
